🔒 Learn About Phishing

Phishing is a social engineering attack that tricks users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details. Always stay alert and think before you click.

⚠️ How to identify phishing attempts

  • 🔍 Look for unusual domains, misspellings, or extra hyphens.
  • 📩 Be cautious with links that ask you to verify or confirm accounts.
  • 🔒 Check for HTTPS, but remember: HTTPS alone does not guarantee safety.
  • 🚫 Never enter credentials on pages reached via unsolicited messages.

✅ Best Practices

  • ⌨️ Type trusted URLs directly into your browser.
  • 🔑 Use multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • 🛡️ Keep browsers and antivirus software up to date.
